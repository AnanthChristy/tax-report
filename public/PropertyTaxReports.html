Property Tax Reports

Page Content

 

 

The County of Santa Clara Department of Tax and Collections Property Tax Reports service is a free service that is solely intended to provide participants property tax-related information of general interest.

 

To subscribe to the Property Tax Reports Service, please enter an email address. 

 

The County shall not be liable for any loss or inconvenience caused by the use of this service or the failure of the service to provide timely response or even the total failure of the service to provide any response at all, whether or not the County is at fault. Storage of contact information, including but not limited to names, telephone numbers, and email addresses in the DTAC request database may constitute a public record which may be subject to disclosure under the California Public Records Act, Government Code Section 6250.

 

  

Returning Users get your download link here


The Reports are updated everyday. You will receive a mail along with the download link and detailed instructions on how to download the file.

New Subscribers register here


The Report will be available in Plain-text(.txt file) format.
Multiple reports will be zipped(.zip folder) and available for download.
